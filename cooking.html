Squash Soup Recipe

Squash soup is good to make when it's cold out, especially if you add a bit of spice to it.

  • 1 large squash
  • 2 qt chicken stock
  • 1 onion diced
  • 2 celery diced
  • 3 cloves garlic minced
  • 1t thyme
  • 2 10oz cans of coconut milk
  • 1T ginger
  • 1/4t cayenne pepper
  • salt and pepper to taste
